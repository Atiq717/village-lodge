The First Indian Restaurant In The Village

The restaurant first opened in July 2008 and on the 30th September 2016 had a refurbishment. We are very proud of our growing reputation and the large amount of repeat custom we attract.

We are very proud to have been awarded 5 star food hygiene rating by South Staffordshire Council.

The Village Lodge is unlicensed, but diners may bring their own alcoholic drinks to the restaurant.

The restaurant seats up to 64 diners and is fully air-conditioned.
